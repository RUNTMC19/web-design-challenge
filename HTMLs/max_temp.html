Max Temperature


Based on the plot, coordinates between -20 to 20 have the highest temperatures as they are closest to the equator (O degrees Latitude). The southern hemisphere coordinates (below 0 degrees Latitude) however have a much higher cluster of high temperatures compare to those in the north (positive degrees Latitude). This is likely due to the winter season where it tends to be colder farther north in the Earth.
